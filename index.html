About Me

I am a PhD candidate specializing in Electrical & Computer Engineering with a focus on Battery Management Systems, Energy Management Systems, and Control Systems for electric vehicles. My research integrates deep learning techniques with control applications to optimize energy distribution and battery state-of-charge estimation.
